About LEADGEN-AI

LEADGEN-AI is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to help users identify potential B2B leads based on industry, location, job title, and company size. It generates personalized outreach emails using either company domains or LinkedIn profiles.

Features include:

  • Domain-based or LinkedIn-based prospecting
  • Personalized cold email generation
  • Lead filtering by industry, country, and role
  • Data enrichment and contact email discovery
